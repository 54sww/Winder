Winder Release Notes

0.3.0 Road Map

* Large scale job scheduler. With quartz, the maximum job scheduling task in one second is around 300 trigger
  even in a 20 instances quartz cluster + mysql.
  Winder will target to rise this ceiling.

* Internal crontab and scheduler support
* Web management

0.1.0

* Multiple steps support for quartz job
